Over the last 4 years our family has traveled a lot and seen many sights. Below you'll see two of the many places we've visited.

Zoos and Aquariums

Zoos and Aquariums are good places for people of all ages to be educated about wildlife and conservation. With wildlife animals coming extint quicker each year, it's important to let the next generations know how important they are to our enviroment. We try to take pictures of the animals along with their name and decription placards. Sometimes it is a little tougher with a toddler running around so we don't get all of the animals we visit.

Disney World and Other Road Trips

At 9 months little A learned to walk. A little over 10 months she got to go to Disney World. In a time where safe distancing and masks were important, she got to experience what took her mother and father many years to experience. Here's some pictures of the parks and our family experiencing it.
