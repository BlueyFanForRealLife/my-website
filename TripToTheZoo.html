Trips to Various Zoos and Aquariums Around the Country

East to West we got to see a lot of animals.

Zoos and Aquariums are good places for people of all ages to be educated about wildlife and conservation.

With wildlife animals coming extint quicker each year, it's important to let the next generations know how important they are to our enviroment.
