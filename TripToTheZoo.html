Zoos and Aquariums

Zoos and Aquariums are good places for people of all ages to be educated about wildlife and conservation. With wildlife animals coming extint quicker each year, it's important to let the next generations know how important they are to our enviroment. We try to take pictures of the animals along with their name and decription placards. Sometimes it is a little tougher with a toddler running around so we don't get all of the animals we visit.
